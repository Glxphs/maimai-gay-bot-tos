## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how MaiBotKt ("the Bot," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our services. By using the Bot, you consent to the collection, use, and handling of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, you must not use the Bot.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Authentication Information

To enable the Bot to retrieve your maimai data, we collect authentication credentials necessary to access maimai DX NET on your behalf. This may include:

- **Session Cookies:** An active session cookie obtained from maimai DX NET, which you may provide manually via bookmarklet or browser extraction.
- **Login Credentials:** Your maimai DX NET username and password, if you choose to authenticate using the username/password method.

### 2.2 maimai Game Data

Once authenticated, the Bot queries maimai DX NET to retrieve your gameplay data. This may include:

- Player name and profile information
- Play history, scores and chart completion data
- In-game collection such as titles, frames, icons and nameplates
- Album uploaded to maimai DX NET

### 2.3 Discord Metadata

In the course of providing service, we may collect:

- Your Discord User ID
- Your Discord username and display name
- Server and channel IDs where the Bot is invoked
- Command usage logs (timestamp and command type, without message content)

### 2.4 Technical Data

We may collect limited technical information necessary for debugging and service improvement:

- Error logs and exception traces
- Timestamps of API requests to maimai DX NET

**We do not collect:** real names, physical addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or government identification numbers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision:** To authenticate with maimai DX NET, retrieve your game data, and generate and display requested outputs.
- **Session Maintenance:** To maintain your authenticated session so you do not need to re-authenticate for every query until the cookie expires.
- **Service Improvement:** To diagnose technical issues, monitor Bot performance, and improve functionality.
- **Security:** To detect unauthorized access attempts, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Use.

**We do not use your information for:** advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or selling to third parties.

## 4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

### 4.1 Storage of Session Cookies

Your maimai DX NET session cookie is stored in our database and is treated as sensitive authentication material. Access to the database is restricted to authorized personnel only.

### 4.2 Handling of Passwords

If you authenticate using the username/password method:

- Your password is transmitted securely to our backend.
- It is used solely to programmatically authenticate with maimai DX NET and obtain a session cookie.
- **Passwords are not stored in any form and are discarded immediately after the authentication transaction completes.**

### 4.3 Storage of Game Data

Retrieved maimai game data may be cached temporarily to improve response times for repeated queries. Cache duration is typically 24 hours. Cached data is not shared with third parties.

### 4.4 Security Measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

- Access controls and authentication for database and infrastructure
- Regular security reviews and dependency updates

**Despite these measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you provide information at your own risk.**

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Legal Compliance:** If required to do so by law, subpoena, court order, or other valid legal process.
- **Protection of Rights:** To enforce our Terms of Use, protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.
- **Service Providers:** To trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating the Bot (e.g., cloud hosting providers), solely to the extent necessary to provide the service, and subject to confidentiality obligations.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Session Cookies:** Retained until they expire naturally, you revoke access, or you request deletion.
- **Game Data:** Retained in cache for approximately 24 hours or until you request deletion.
- **Discord Metadata:** Retained for 30 days.
- **Technical Logs:** Retained for 7 days for debugging purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time as described in Section 7.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your stored session cookie, cached game data, and associated records. To request deletion, contact us at the provided contact information.
- **Revocation:** Revoke the Bot's access by invalidating your maimai DX NET session (e.g., logging out of maimai DX NET on all devices or changing your password).
- **Objection:** Object to certain types of processing, where applicable under local law.

We will respond to verifiable requests within 30 days.
